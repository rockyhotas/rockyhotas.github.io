Product between a complex number $z_1$ and its conjugate $z_1^*$:

$$z_1 = a + ib\\
z_2 = a - ib = z_1^*\\
z_3 = z_1 \cdot z_2 = a^2 + b^2 = \left| z_1 \right|^2\\
a,b \in \mathbb{R}$$
